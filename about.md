# About Hian Kup Khoi

Hian Kup Khoi is a comprehensive resource center dedicated to making data analysis, econometrics, and research methodology accessible to Lao-speaking researchers and students.

## Our Mission

We believe that high-quality education in quantitative research should be available to everyone, regardless of language barriers. Our mission is to provide clear, practical guidance in Lao language for:

- Data analysis and statistical methods
- Econometric modeling
- Geospatial analysis
- Research methodology

## What We Offer

### Foundational Statistics
Master the fundamentals of statistical analysis, hypothesis testing, and probability theory.

### Data Analysis using Stata
Learn to use Stata for data manipulation, statistical analysis, and visualization. From basic commands to advanced econometric techniques.

### Data Analysis using R
Discover the power of R programming for reproducible research, data visualization, and statistical computing.

### Publications
Access our research publications, working papers, and technical guides.

### Activities
Join our seminars, workshops, and training sessions on various topics in data analysis and research.

## Why Hian Kup Khoi?

- **Language Accessibility**: All content is provided in Lao language
- **Practical Focus**: Real-world examples with reproducible code and datasets
- **Comprehensive**: From beginner to advanced level content
- **Research-Oriented**: Designed for academic and applied research needs
